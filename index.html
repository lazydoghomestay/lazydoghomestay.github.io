ABOUT LAZY DOG HOMESTAY

A cozy pet-friendly private cabin house located at the quiet side of Baguio only minutes from downtown.
Located 2km from Session Road and Burnham Park at Amparro Heights. Roughly around 10-15 minutes away by car or taxi.
We are also located along a Jeepney line for commuting travelers or if you prefer to leave your car at the cabin house.
We have 2 huge secured lawns for your pets and kids to play at with an overlooking view of Baguio's Camp John Hay forest.
LDH is perfect for large groups with pets and groups looking for a relaxing peaceful and quiet environment.
For P600 per person per day you get unlimited eggs, unlimited fresh veggies,
organic home grown mushrooms and unlimited brewed coffee with 8 variants to choose from.
Food is FREE at Lazy Dog Homestay!

Behold OUR ROOMS

We have 3 rooms in the cabin house that can accommodate a total of 15-20 people. Each room can fit 3-5 pax in 2 double beds and 1 single bed per room. Optional floor mattresses can be requested. Our attic room has 5 single beds and is a smoking room. We have 2 private bathrooms for guests use only cleaned regularly. Hot and cold shower is available. Rooms are non smoking rooms except for the attic room. Linens and beddings are guaranteed to be clean and fresh changed every new set of check-in unlike other affordable home stays. Floors are sanitized and mopped with color safe bleach every time a new set of guest checks in. Cleanliness is our utmost priority in LDH!

Your food is on us! Eat it, it's free!
We offer FREE eggs, fresh Baguio veggies, organic oyster mushrooms and unlimited french pressed coffee all for free. Not bad for P600 bucks per person per day.

We have 2 huge secured gardens for your pets and kids to play at. LDH has an overlooking view of Camp John Hay's forest reserve. We also have an outdoor barbecue patio area and a bonfire pit.

Entertainment

Theater like experience with our 4200 ansi lumens PROJECTOR setup for Netflix movies, PS4 or Xbox gaming. Outdoor movie setup can also be setup if weather is good.

Free High Speed Wifi

Housekeeping

Rooms and linens guaranteed to be clean and sanitized compared to other affordable home stays. Laundry area also free to use for guests.

Restaurants Nearby

Too lazy to cook? No problem! We have several famous restaurants nearby and a grocery store with a good collection of wines, booze, cheese, etc.

Secured Indoor Parking

Indoor parking for 3 cars available. No need to risk your car from parking fines when parked by the street. We have a 3 car garage.
